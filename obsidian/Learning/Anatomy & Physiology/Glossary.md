# NASM A&P — Master Glossary

> A–Z reference for all key terms from the NASM Anatomy, Physiology & Kinesiology course.
> Jump to a letter or use Cmd+F to search.

---

## A

- **Abduction** — movement AWAY from midline (frontal plane) → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Frontal Plane Movements|Frontal Plane]]
- **Acetylcholine** — neurotransmitter released at neuromuscular junction → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Excitation-Contraction Coupling|Excitation-Contraction Coupling]]
- **Actin** — thin filament in muscle sarcomere (contractile protein) → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Structure|Muscle Structure]]
- **Adduction** — movement TOWARD midline (frontal plane)
- **Adenosine Triphosphate (ATP)** — body's energy currency → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems|Bioenergetics]]
- **Aerobic** — requires oxygen
- **Afferent pathway** — sensory signals going UP to brain → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Pathways|Nervous System Pathways]]
- **Agonist** — prime mover muscle → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Roles|Muscle Roles]]
- **All-or-nothing principle** — motor unit either fires completely or not at all → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Motor Units & the All-or-Nothing Principle|Motor Units]]
- **Alveoli** — air sacs in lungs where gas exchange occurs (via diffusion) → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System#Gas Exchange — The Full Process|Gas Exchange]]
- **Anaerobic** — without oxygen
- **Anatomy** — study of body structure
- **Angiogenesis** — formation of new capillaries from existing vessels
- **Anterior** — front of body
- **Antagonist** — muscle opposing prime mover → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Roles|Muscle Roles]]
- **Aorta** — largest artery in body
- **Appendicular skeleton** — arms, legs, pelvic girdle (~126 bones) → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Two Divisions of the Skeleton|Skeletal Divisions]]
- **Arteries** — blood vessels carrying blood AWAY from heart
- **Arterioles** — smaller branches of arteries
- **Arthrokinematics** — joint movements inside the joint (roll, slide, spin) → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Arthrokinematics vs Osteokinematics|Arthrokinematics]]
- **Atrium** (plural: atria) — smaller upper chambers of heart → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System#Four Chambers, Two Pumps|Heart Chambers]]
- **ATP-PCr system** — phosphagen energy pathway (0-10 sec, anaerobic) → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems#System 1: ATP-PCr (Phosphagen System)|ATP-PCr]]
- **Autonomic NS** — involuntary (sympathetic + parasympathetic) → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#PNS Further Subdivisions|Autonomic NS]]
- **Axial skeleton** — skull, ribcage, spine (~80 bones)
- **Axon** — part of neuron sending signals OUT

---

## B

- **Bioenergetics** — study of energy conversion in the body → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems|Bioenergetics]]
- **Biomechanics** — study of forces on the body and their effect on movement → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology|Biomechanics]]
- **Blood pressure** — measurement of blood against artery walls → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System#Blood Pressure|Blood Pressure]]
- **Bradycardia** — resting HR below 60 bpm
- **Bradypnea** — breathing rate below 8/min

---

## C

- **Capillaries** — smallest vessels, gas exchange sites
- **Capillarisation** — formation of new capillaries (training adaptation)
- **Cardiac muscle** — involuntary heart muscle → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Three Types of Muscle|Muscle Types]]
- **Cardiac output (Q)** — HR × SV, blood pumped per minute → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System#Key Heart Measurements|Heart Measurements]]
- **Cardiorespiratory** — cardiovascular + respiratory together → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System|Cardiorespiratory System]]
- **Central nervous system (CNS)** — brain + spinal cord → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Two Main Divisions|NS Divisions]]
- **Cervical** — neck region (C1-C7) → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#The Vertebral Column|Vertebral Column]]
- **Concentric** — muscle shortening (acceleration) → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#The Full Spectrum|Muscle Actions]]
- **Condyles** — bony projections (femur/tibia form knee)
- **Contralateral** — opposite side of body

---

## D

- **Dendrites** — receive signals into neuron
- **Diaphragm** — primary breathing muscle → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System#Respiratory Muscles|Respiratory Muscles]]
- **Diastolic** — BP when heart relaxes
- **Diffusion** — gas exchange mechanism in alveoli
- **Distal** — further from centre
- **Dorsiflexion** — foot toward shin (sagittal plane)

---

## E

- **Eccentric** — muscle lengthening (deceleration, "negative") → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#The Full Spectrum|Muscle Actions]]
- **Efferent pathway** — motor signals going DOWN from CNS
- **Elastin** — protein in ligaments providing flexibility
- **Epimysium** — deep fascia wrapping whole muscle → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Structure|Muscle Structure]]
- **Eversion** — arch collapses inward (frontal plane)
- **Excitation-contraction coupling** — process of muscle contraction → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Excitation-Contraction Coupling|Full Process]]
- **Expiration** — breathing out
- **Extension** — joint angle increases (straightening)

---

## F

- **Fascia** — connective tissue surrounding muscle
- **Fascicle** — bundle of muscle fibres
- **Feed forward activation** — stabilisers contracting automatically in anticipation → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Feed Forward Activation|Feed Forward]]
- **Flexion** — joint angle decreases (bending)
- **Frontal plane** — divides body into front/back halves (coronal plane) → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Planes of Motion|Planes]]
- **Fulcrum** — pivot point of lever → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Levers in the Human Body|Levers]]

---

## G

- **Gliding joint** — plane joint, side-to-side (wrist) → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Six Types of Synovial Joints|Joint Types]]
- **Glycolysis** — anaerobic energy pathway using glucose (30-60 sec) → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems#System 2: Glycolysis|Glycolysis]]
- **Golgi Tendon Organ (GTO)** — senses muscle tension → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Mechanoreceptors — The Three Critical Types|Mechanoreceptors]]

---

## H

- **Heart rate (HR)** — beats per minute
- **Hinge joint** — door-like movement (elbow, knee)
- **Homeostasis** — maintaining internal balance
- **Hyperextension** — extension beyond normal limit
- **Hypertension** — high blood pressure

---

## I

- **Inferior** — below
- **Inspiration** — breathing in (inhalation)
- **Interneurons** — transmit within CNS only
- **Intervertebral disc** — fibrous cartilage shock absorber → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#The Vertebral Column|Vertebral Column]]
- **Inversion** — foot rolls to outer edge (frontal plane)
- **Ipsilateral** — same side of body
- **Isometric** — muscle maintains length (stabilisation) → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#The Full Spectrum|Muscle Actions]]

---

## J

- **Joint** — junction between bones → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Joints — Classification|Joint Classification]]
- **Joint capsule** — surrounds synovial joint
- **Joint receptors** — sense pressure, acceleration in joint → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Mechanoreceptors — The Three Critical Types|Mechanoreceptors]]

---

## K

- **Kinesiology** — study of human movement → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology|Biomechanics & Kinesiology]]
- **Kinetic chain** — nervous + skeletal + muscular systems → [[Kinetic Chain/Kinetic Chain|Kinetic Chain]]

---

## L

- **Lactic acid** — by-product of anaerobic glycolysis → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems#System 2: Glycolysis|Glycolysis]]
- **Lactic threshold** — where lactate accumulates faster than cleared → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems#Cardiorespiratory Fitness Metrics|Fitness Metrics]]
- **Lateral** — away from midline
- **Ligament** — connects BONE to BONE → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Ligaments|Ligaments]]
- **Long bones** — cylindrical shaft (femur)
- **Lumbar** — lower back (L1-L5)

---

## M

- **Mechanoreceptors** — respond to touch, pressure, motion → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Mechanoreceptors — The Three Critical Types|Mechanoreceptors]]
- **Medial** — toward midline
- **Mitochondria** — cellular powerhouse
- **Mitral valve** — between left atrium and left ventricle
- **Motor learning stages** — cognitive, associative, autonomous → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Motor Skill Learning — Three Stages|Motor Learning]]
- **Motor neuron** — nerve cell controlling muscle → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Neural Activation — How Muscles Contract|Neural Activation]]
- **Motor unit** — one motor neuron + all fibres it innervates
- **Multifidus** — deep spinal stabiliser
- **Muscle spindle** — senses muscle length → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Mechanoreceptors — The Three Critical Types|Mechanoreceptors]]
- **Myofibril** — contractile unit within muscle fibre
- **Myoglobin** — oxygen-binding protein (high in red/slow-twitch fibres)
- **Myosin** — thick filament in muscle sarcomere → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Structure|Muscle Structure]]

---

## N

- **Nervous system** — communication network (CNS + PNS) → [[Nervous System/Nervous System|Nervous System]]
- **Neuromuscular junction** — connection between nerve and muscle
- **Neuroplasticity** — brain's ability to change and form new connections → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Motor Skill Learning — Three Stages|Motor Learning]]
- **Neurotransmitters** — chemical messengers at synapse
- **Neutral spine** — optimal S-curve position → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#The Vertebral Column|Vertebral Column]]
- **Non-synovial joint** — immovable (skull sutures)

---

## O

- **Osteoblast** — cell that BUILDS bone ("Blasts Build") → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Bone Remodeling|Bone Remodeling]]
- **Osteoclast** — cell that breaks DOWN bone ("Clasts Crush")
- **Osteokinematics** — visible joint movements → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Arthrokinematics vs Osteokinematics|Arthrokinematics]]
- **Osteoporosis** — reduced bone density
- **Oxidative system** — aerobic energy pathway (2+ min) → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems#System 3: Oxidative System|Oxidative System]]
- **Oxygen uptake** — body's use of oxygen

---

## P

- **Parasympathetic NS** — "rest and digest" → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#PNS Further Subdivisions|Autonomic NS]]
- **Peripheral nervous system (PNS)** — nerves outside CNS
- **Phosphagen system** — see ATP-PCr → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems#System 1: ATP-PCr (Phosphagen System)|ATP-PCr]]
- **Phosphocreatine (PCr)** — stored in muscle for rapid ATP
- **Planes of motion** — sagittal, frontal, transverse → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Planes of Motion|Planes of Motion]]
- **Plantar flexion** — foot points down
- **Plasma** — watery part of blood
- **Platelets** — blood component for clotting
- **Pronation (forearm)** — palm down
- **Pronation (foot)** — multi-plane: eversion + dorsiflexion + abduction → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Foot — Multiplanar Movement|Foot Movement]]
- **Prone** — lying face DOWN
- **Proprioception** — body's sense of its own position → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#Proprioception|Proprioception]]
- **Proximal** — closer to centre

---

## R

- **Red blood cells** — carry oxygen
- **Respiratory system** — airways + lungs + respiratory muscles → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System#Part 2 — Respiratory System|Respiratory System]]

---

## S

- **Sacrum** — fused triangular bone below lumbar
- **Sagittal plane** — divides body into left/right halves → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Planes of Motion|Planes]]
- **SA node** — sinoatrial node, heart's pacemaker → [[Cardiorespiratory System/Cardiorespiratory System#Heart's Electrical Conduction System|Conduction System]]
- **Sarcomere** — functional unit of muscle contraction → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Structure|Muscle Structure]]
- **Scapular movements** — retraction, protraction, elevation, depression → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Scapular Movements|Scapular Movements]]
- **Sesamoid bone** — embedded in tendon (patella)
- **Sliding filament theory** — mechanism of muscle contraction → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Excitation-Contraction Coupling|Sliding Filament]]
- **Stabiliser** — muscle preventing unwanted movement → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Roles|Muscle Roles]]
- **Stroke volume (SV)** — blood pumped per heartbeat
- **Supination (forearm)** — palm up
- **Supination (foot)** — multi-plane: inversion + plantar flexion + adduction
- **Supine** — lying face UP
- **Sympathetic NS** — "fight or flight" → [[Nervous System/Nervous System#PNS Further Subdivisions|Autonomic NS]]
- **Synergist** — muscle assisting prime mover → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Roles|Muscle Roles]]
- **Synovial joint** — freely moveable joint → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Six Types of Synovial Joints|Joint Types]]
- **Synovial fluid** — joint lubricant

---

## T

- **Tachycardia** — HR above 100 bpm
- **Tachypnea** — breathing rate above 24/min
- **Tendon** — connects MUSCLE to BONE → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Tendons vs Ligaments|Tendons vs Ligaments]]
- **Thoracic** — mid-back (T1-T12)
- **Transversus abdominis (TVA)** — deep core stabiliser → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Feed Forward Activation|Feed Forward Activation]]
- **Transverse plane** — divides body into top/bottom halves → [[Biomechanics & Kinesiology/Biomechanics & Kinesiology#Transverse Plane Movements|Transverse Plane]]
- **Type I fibres** — slow twitch (endurance, red) → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Fibre Types|Fibre Types]]
- **Type II fibres** — fast twitch (power, white)

---

## V

- **Veins** — carry blood back to heart
- **Vena cava (superior/inferior)** — major veins returning to right atrium
- **Ventricle** — larger lower chamber of heart
- **Vertebral column** — the spine → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#The Vertebral Column|Vertebral Column]]
- **VO2 max** — maximum oxygen uptake → [[Bioenergetics & Energy Systems/Bioenergetics & Energy Systems#Cardiorespiratory Fitness Metrics|Fitness Metrics]]

---

## W

- **White blood cells** — fight infection
- **Weight-bearing exercise** — optimal for bone health → [[Skeletal System/Skeletal System#Exercise & Bone Health|Bone Health]]

---

## Z

- **Z-line** — boundary between sarcomeres → [[Muscular System/Muscular System#Muscle Structure|Muscle Structure]]
